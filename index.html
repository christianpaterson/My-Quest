Who is Chris Quest? What does he stand for?

My name is Christian Paterson, but sometimes I like to call myself "Chris Quest."

Read on to find out what I value most, and what motivates the actions that make up my life's Quest.

So, what do I care about?

The 10 things that are most important to me are...

  1. Living well (enjoyment + impact)
  2. Seeing things as they are, intending well, and upholding honesty
  3. Understanding, forgiving, and disciplining myself
  4. Positively affecting my loved ones
  5. Giving maximum (or close to maximum) effort
  6. Staying focused; not allowing our noisy world to dictate my concerns
  7. Developing myself professionally
  8. Learning WEB DEV ;)
  9. Getting stronger, and exercising often
  10. High quality rest, and sleeping enough

Some other important things to me are humility, balance, and resiliance.

I like to think on the buddhist 8-fold path:

First is simply seeing things as they are: Right Understanding(1)

Then is not poluting reality by fueling our unhelpful thoughts: Right Thought(2)

Finally, we try to resist the urge to distort things with lies: Right Speech(3)

I group Right Action(4), Right Livelihood(5), & Right Effort(6) together into broadly living well.

Finally Right Mindfulness(7) & Right Concentration(8) enhance our lives as we remain focused and open to the truth.
